Join Our Nonviolent Community

Nonviolent Austin is dedicated to promoting peace and nonviolence in our community through:

  • Kingian Nonviolence training and workshops
  • Community outreach and education programs
  • Peaceful conflict resolution practices
  • Building a network of trained nonviolence practitioners

Together we can expand nonviolence education throughout Austin's libraries, schools, and places of worship.

Our Mission

Nonviolent Austin is part of Pace e Bene's Campaign Nonviolence, working to end war, poverty, racism, and environmental destruction while building a culture of active nonviolence. In the spirit of St. Francis, Gandhi, Dorothy Day, and Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr., we invite you to join us in walking the path of nonviolence.
