Join Us in Making History

Six dedicated members of Nonviolent Austin are preparing for a transformative journey to Selma, Alabama. They will:

  • Train in Kingian Nonviolence with Civil Rights leader Dr. Bernard Lafayette
  • Participate in the 60th anniversary of Bloody Sunday
  • Walk across the historic Edmund Pettus Bridge
  • Bring vital nonviolence training skills back to Austin

Your support will help expand nonviolence education throughout Austin's libraries, schools, and places of worship.

Our Mission

Nonviolent Austin is part of Pace e Bene's Campaign Nonviolence, working to end war, poverty, racism, and environmental destruction while building a culture of active nonviolence. In the spirit of St. Francis, Gandhi, Dorothy Day, and Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr., we invite you to join us in walking the path of nonviolence.
